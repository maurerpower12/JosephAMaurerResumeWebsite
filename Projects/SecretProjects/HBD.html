~Happy B-Day Bro~
Dear Jim,

Happy b-day to the most loving, caring, and compassionate brother a man could ask for. Over the years you've shown me what it means to inspire and be inspired through the things you make and the time you spend crafting them. Yet through that time, I don't know if I've ever made something for you in the language that I find most expressive. So here it is. A mismatch of Javascript and HTML working concurrently to create a color percussion. Why did I make you a website page filled with over 1000+ randomly generated objects?

  1. It's objectively awesome.
  2. I want to make you feel excited about your birthday.

I often think back about the excitement we had as kids around our birthdays. Yours and Kirsten's birthday always seemed to capture an eagerness and excitement that was unrivaled at any other time of the year. While you might not be on the beach or at a baseball this year, I hope you look back on those memories and remember what it felt like to be excited and to have a vast world of possibilities ahead of you. And who doesn't get excited about a countdown to fireworks?

Love you, brother
Lit' Bro
